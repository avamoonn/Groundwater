How to Use DFPS

Getting Started

To use DFPS, click the Green Run DFPS Button. Then, use the form to enter appropriate values. Use the specified units provided on the input page, and the website will make necessary conversions.

Press Submit, and results will appear below the input form.

Explanation of Outputs

Fraction of Pumping Over Time

Shows the fraction of water pumped that originates from the stream over time.

Stream Flow Rate Over Time

Displays the stream's flow rate as it decreases due to pumping over time.

Drawdown at Observation Well 1

Represents how the water table is lowered at a specific observation well over time.

Drawdown at Observation Well 2

Displays the lowering of the water table at another observation well location.

W-E Cross-Section of Drawdown

Shows the drawdown variation along the east-west direction at a specific time.

S-N Cross-Section of Drawdown

Illustrates the drawdown variation along the north-south direction at a specific time.

Contour Map of Drawdown

A 2D map displaying spatial drawdown at a specific time.
